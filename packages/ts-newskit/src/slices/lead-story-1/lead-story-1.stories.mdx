# LeadStory1 component
The `LeadStory1` component is used to display the main story, supplementary articles and comment quotes.
It is comprised of the `leadArticle`, `ArticleListItem`, `smallArticles`, `groupedArticles`, `singleArticle`, and `articlesWithListItems` slice components.

This takes in a series of props, as below, to display the part of the slices.
